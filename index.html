Current Research Projects

Autonomous Data-Driven Modelling for Advanced Satellite Constellation Management
Developing autonomous, data-driven models for satellite constellation management using digital twin technology. The research focuses on creating highly accurate virtual models of satellite systems, integrating historical and real-time telemetry data for enhanced predictive maintenance and operational efficiency. This work aims to address satellite degradation challenges through advanced machine learning techniques and continuous trajectory modeling.
Digital Twins Machine Learning Satellite Systems Predictive Maintenance Telemetry Data
Dynamic Modular Approach to System Architectures
Exploring the relationship between lifecycle modularity and lifecycle costs across the product lifecycle. This research encompasses all aspects of a product's lifecycle, from initial conceptual design through normal use to disposal, using a cradle-to-grave design philosophy applied to consumer products.
Product Modularity Lifecycle Costing Systems Architecture Design for Assembly Manufacturing
